About the Artist

Elysia Ortiz is a contemporary visual artist whose work exists at the intersection of emotion, memory, and abstraction. Each piece is designed not simply to be seen, but to be felt.

Working with layered texture and tonal contrast, Elysia creates visual narratives that explore identity, silence, and internal movement.

“Art is not what you look at — it is what you begin to feel when you stop looking away.”
